About

Who am I.

I love to make cool stuff. Currently I'm studing Mechanical Engineering at San Diego State University with minors in Computer Science and Energy Studies. As an assistant researcher at Dr. Kong's Electrochemical Energy Lab I'm helping to develop advanced solid-state electrolytes for industrial applications.

Growing up I would take apart and put back together anything I could find and eventually that spiraled into me making my own projects from scratch weather it be 3D printed, woodworking, coded or anything else I love make a project that I can look back on and say that I built it from the ground up. My passion lended it's self to Robotics in my youth and now that passion has turned me towards the direction of renewables engineering.

Outside school and the lab I work part time teaching elementary students STEM topics via Kodely.
